## Description
*(≤4000 characters)*

```
Take a Break helps you protect your eyes and reduce fatigue during long work sessions. It runs quietly in your menu bar and automatically shows a full-screen break reminder on a schedule you control — no manual timers, no willpower needed.

HOW IT WORKS

Two independent timers run in the background:

• Micro break (eye rest) — every 15 minutes, a short 15-second overlay prompts you to look away and rest your eyes.
• Macro break (long break) — every 60 minutes, a 5-minute full-screen break gives your eyes, neck, and shoulders a proper rest.

When a break fires, a full-screen overlay covers your display so you can step away with zero distractions. A live countdown shows how long remains. You can skip or stop a break early if you need to finish something urgent.

SMART SCHEDULING

Take a Break is designed to stay out of your way. If a micro break is due within 45 seconds of an upcoming macro break, the micro break is skipped automatically — no double interruptions. After each macro break, both timers reset from zero so you always start fresh.

FULLY CUSTOMISABLE

Every duration is adjustable from the Settings panel:

• How long to work before each micro break
• How long each micro break lasts
• How long to work before each macro break
• How long each macro break lasts

Changes take effect immediately — no restart needed.

OVERLAY STYLES

Choose the break overlay that suits you:
• Simple — a clean, dark full-screen overlay
• Animation — a subtle animated overlay to help you relax

LIVE COUNTDOWN

Open Settings to see a live countdown to your next micro and macro break. Always know exactly how much working time you have left.

ONE-TAP PAUSE

Pause all breaks with a single toggle — useful for presentations, video calls, or focused sprints. Resume with one tap and your timers restart from zero.

LIGHTWEIGHT & ALWAYS ON

Take a Break uses no background processing beyond a simple 1-second timer. It launches at login automatically so you never have to remember to start it. No accounts, no subscriptions, no internet connection required.

PRIVACY

Take a Break collects no data, connects to no servers, and requires no permissions beyond running on your Mac.
```
