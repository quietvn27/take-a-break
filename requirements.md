Create an IOS app that can run on Macbook, named "Take a break".

Basic features:
- Every 5 minutes, it will add an overlay image on the screen so that users cannot interact with anything on the screen. Users have to take 15 seconds off to relax their eyes. Then they can work on the laptop again.
- Every 20 minutes, it forces users to take a break for 5 minutes.
- The duration of working and resting and can be configured.
- During the break time, there's a button users can click to stop the break and keep working.
